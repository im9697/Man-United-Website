About

Manchester United, (AKA "Red Devils") is one of the greatest clubs of all time as they have great legends such as Cristiano Ronaldo, Wayne Rooney, Eric Cantona and many more. The stadium of Manchester United is called "Old Trafford" which is located in Manchester, England. Old Trafford is known as "The Theatre Of Dreams". It is known as The Theatre Of Dreams because its the place where you follow your dreams, glory and happiness. This is where Manchester United has built their heart winning history.

History

Manchester United has won many trophies , have many legends and have had many unforgettable games. The club has won 13 Premier League titles, 3 Champions League titles and 13 FA cups. Their rivalries are Liverpool being the top rival, Manchester City and Arsenal. Manchester United has made awesome comebacks and goals mostly against these clubs.
